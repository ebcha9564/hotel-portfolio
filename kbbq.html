NOKNAMU (녹나무)

Galbi House of LongStanding Tradition

Starting as Noknamu in 1984, Nakwon has grown into a renowned galbi (Korean BBQ ribs) establishment beloved by three generations. Known for its signature sauce and proprietary rib aging method, Nakwon consistently delivers its original flavor. The light and refreshing Pyongyang-style naengmyeon (cold noodles) perfectly complement the savory galbi.
